Privacy Policy

BitLut is an Android utility app that helps synchronize fitness activity data from Huawei Health to Android Health Connect after explicit user authorization.

Last updated: May 2026

1. What BitLut does

BitLut is designed to transfer selected fitness and health activity data between Huawei Health and Android Health Connect. The app works only after the user grants the required permissions.

2. Data BitLut may access

Depending on the permissions granted by the user, BitLut may access:

  • step count;
  • heart rate data;
  • synchronization timestamps;
  • technical diagnostic logs generated locally by the app.

3. How the data is used

BitLut uses this data only to:

  • synchronize activity data between Huawei Health and Android Health Connect;
  • show synchronization status inside the app;
  • help diagnose synchronization problems when the user chooses to review or copy logs.

4. Local processing

BitLut is designed to process synchronization data locally on the user’s device whenever possible. The app does not use advertising SDKs and does not sell personal data.

5. Third-party services

To provide synchronization, BitLut interacts with:

  • Huawei Health / HMS Health Kit;
  • Android Health Connect.

These services may process data according to their own privacy policies and user settings.

6. User control

Users can revoke permissions at any time through Android system settings, Huawei Health settings, or Health Connect settings. Users can also stop using BitLut by uninstalling the app.

7. Data subject rights

Users may request information about the data handled by BitLut or ask questions about privacy by contacting the project through GitHub.

Repository: github.com/spntext-jpg/BitLut

8. Medical disclaimer

BitLut is not a medical application. It does not provide diagnosis, treatment, medical advice, or interpretation of health conditions. The app is intended only for personal activity tracking and data synchronization.
